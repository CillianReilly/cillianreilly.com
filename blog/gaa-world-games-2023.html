gaa world games 2023
 sport 

The GAA World Games 2023 took place in Derry, Northern Ireland between 24th and 28th July. I was picked to represent Asia in the Men's Open Football tournament (open to all players of an appropriate skill level, Irish or otherwise). A great honour to be picked and a fantastic week of GAA to be involved in. Asia Men's were the eventual shield winners.

The week in full is covered in more detail on the Singapore Gaelic Lions website: GAA World Games 2023, which I also maintain.
